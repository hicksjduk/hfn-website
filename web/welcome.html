Adding Credit icons to your web pages

Welcome.

It is now our corporate policy to ask that all web space customers include a Credit icon recognising Hampshire County Council at the foot of all web pages.

The icon will be held in your 'images' directory, at /images/credit .

You have two icons to choose from :

Blue background :


Please copy the following lines of HTML and add them to the foot of all of your web pages.. 

<p>
  <center>
    <a href="http://www.hants.gov.uk">
      <img src="/images/credit/hosted-blue.gif" align="middle" width ="154" height="70" border=0 alt="Web Space provided by Hampshire County Council">
    </a>
  </center>
</p>

White background :



Please copy the following lines of HTML and add them to the foot of all of your web pages.. 

<p>
  <center>
    <a href="http://www.hants.gov.uk">
      <img src="/images/credit/hosted-white.gif" align="middle" width ="154" height="70" border=0 alt="Web Space provided by Hampshire County Council">
    </a>
  </center>
</p>

Thank you.
